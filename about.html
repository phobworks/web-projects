About Us

Delivering measurable value across engineering, logistics, and support operations.

Welcome to the world of INTEGRATED Energy Services Limited! Truly, great things start from small beginnings; ours has been a history of sure and steady growth

From a share capital of N100,000.00 (One hundred thousand Naira) at inception on 4th of December, 1996, to a working capital of over N250,000,000 (Two Hundred and Fifty Million Naira), some Twenty-Four years later. From a mere supplier of manpower to a renowned supplier of qualified manpower for the oil/gas drilling and production Industry. From a stockist and supplier of foreign goods sourced locally to a major supplier of foreign based goods sourced abroad and manufacturers' representatives. From minor maintenance and engineering outfit for the Downstream sector to a major maintenance, engineering, construction and marine transportation outfit carved particularly for the Nigerian Industry.

This is the IESL success story!

Our Core Values

Integrated Energy Services Limited (IESL) prioritizes customer-centricity, safety, integrity, respect for people, professionalism, and excellence in their core values. These values guide their dealings with stakeholders and inform their corporate culture.

Customer-Centricity: IESL is dedicated to fulfilling customer needs and building strong relationships.

Safety: Prioritizing safety for employees, contractors, local communities, and the environment is crucial.

Integrity: Honesty and ethical conduct are fundamental to their operations.

Respect for People: Valuing and respecting all stakeholders is a core principle.

Professionalism:Maintaining high standards of conduct and expertise is essential.

Excellence: Striving for exceptional performance and delivering outstanding value is a key focus.
